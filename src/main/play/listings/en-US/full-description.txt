This application adds exo Sainte-Julie buses information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from @exo_SteJulie on Twitter.

exo Sainte-Julie serves Beloeil, Longueuil, McMasterville, Montréal, Saint-Bruno-de-Montarville, Sainte-Julie and Sainte-Hyacinthe in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by exo.
https://exo.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-ste-julie-omitsju-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Sainte-Julie.

exo Sainte-Julie was formerly known as RTM Sainte-Julie sector and OMIT de Sainte-Julie.
exo was formerly known as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news (Twitter)
